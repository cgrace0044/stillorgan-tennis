About Us

Our Club

Founded in 1903 Stillorgan Lawn Tennis Club is a highly successful club located in the heart of Stillorgan Village, Dublin, Ireland. We cater for tennis at all levels, both competitive and social, from complete beginners to national champions. We offer family, individual, junior and student memberships. Our teams participate in Dublin Lawn Tennis Leagues from Class 1 to Class 8 levels. Stillorgan Lawn Tennis Club is a very social club with lots of events throughout the year both on court and in our spectacular clubhouse.

Our members enjoy

  • Year-round tennis and social events
  • 10 indoor tennis courts
  • 10 outdoor tennis courts
  • Clubhouse with bar and restaurant
  • Jacuzzi and sauna
  • Club championships
  • Adult and junior socials
  • Tennis lessons for all levels
